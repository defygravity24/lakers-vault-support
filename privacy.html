Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Lakers Vault is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect — and the short answer is: none.

Data Collection

Lakers Vault does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device and does not require an account or login to use.

We do not collect:

• Names, email addresses, or contact information

• Location data

• Usage analytics or behavioral data

• Device identifiers

• Any other personal information

Internet Connection

Lakers Vault does not require an internet connection. All content and images are bundled locally within the app. No data is sent to external servers.

Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data to share. There are no ads, no tracking SDKs, and no analytics services in this app.
